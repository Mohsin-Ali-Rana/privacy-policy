Privacy Policy for FlashShare

Last Updated: January 03, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FlashShare ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. FlashShare is designed as an Offline File Transfer tool; user data is transferred directly between devices via a local connection (Wi-Fi/Hotspot) and is not uploaded to our servers.

1. Information Collection and Use

To provide the core functionality of FlashShare (File Transfer, Phone Clone, and FlashCast), we require access to specific data on your device. This data is processed locally and only transferred to devices you explicitly connect to.

A. Files and Media (Storage)

Permissions: READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

Purpose: Allows selecting and saving files during transfers.

Data Handling: Files are accessed only when you initiate a transfer. No indexing or analysis is performed.

B. Contacts

Permissions: READ_CONTACTS, WRITE_CONTACTS

Purpose: Used exclusively for the Phone Clone feature.

Data Handling: Contacts are temporarily processed to create a backup file and are never uploaded to any server.

C. Installed Apps

Permission: QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES

Purpose: Enables listing installed apps for sharing or cloning.

Data Handling: App usage is not tracked or analyzed.

D. Location and Local Network

Permissions: ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION, NEARBY_WIFI_DEVICES, ACCESS_WIFI_STATE

Purpose: Required by Android to discover Wi-Fi networks and establish local connections.

Data Handling: No GPS location is stored, tracked, or shared.

E. Camera

Permission: CAMERA

Purpose: Used only for scanning QR codes to establish device connections.

Data Handling: Camera data is processed in real time and never saved.

2. How We Transfer Data

FlashShare operates on a Peer-to-Peer (P2P) architecture using a local HTTP server and WebSockets.

  • Data transfers directly between sender and receiver.
  • No internet or third-party servers are involved.

3. Data Security

  • PIN Protection: Optional session PIN for secure connections.
  • Local-Only Processing: Data stays within your local network.
  • Transient Storage: Temporary files may be cleared after transfer.

  • Google Play Services

5. Children’s Privacy

FlashShare does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.
